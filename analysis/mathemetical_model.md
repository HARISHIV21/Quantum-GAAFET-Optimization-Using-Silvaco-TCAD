# Mathematical Model

## Gate Oxide Capacitance

Cox = εox / tox

Higher oxide capacitance improves gate control over the channel.

---

## Drain Current

ID = μCox(W/L)[(VGS − VTH)VDS − VDS²/2]

Represents the current flowing through the transistor during operation.

---

## ON/OFF Current Ratio

ION / IOFF

A higher ratio indicates better digital switching performance.

---

## Subthreshold Swing

SS = dVGS / d(log ID)

Lower SS indicates faster switching and lower leakage power.

---

## Physical Models Used

- Fermi-Dirac Statistics
- Quantum Tunneling
- Non-local Band-to-Band Tunneling
- Shockley-Read-Hall Recombination
- Auger Recombination
- Field & Concentration Dependent Mobility
